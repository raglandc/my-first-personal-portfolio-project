About Chris Ragland

Chris Ragland is a sophomore majoring in Computer Science. He became interested in coding during a college class project that showed him how creative coding can be. Chris has worked as an Emergency Medical Technician for several years following his graduation from high school. It was this experience as an EMT that taught Chris the importance of hard work, patience, and working as a team. Chris's goals are to continue his studies in CS and push the boundaries between imagination and what most people consider possible with code. When Chris is not working on code or schoolwork, he enjoys analyzing different financial markets through technical analysis and hanging out with friends.
